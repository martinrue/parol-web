Kio

Kiel

Esperanto kaj la Pola havas similajn sonojn. Parol fonetike transskribas Esperantan tekston en la polan, por ke oni povu uzi profesiajn parolsintezajn sistemojn por paroli Esperanton. Oni povas ŝanĝi la regulojn, kiujn uzas la sistemo por transskribi la tekston, per alklaki la agordbutonon.

What

Parol is an Esperanto speech robot. It takes Esperanto text and speaks it out loud.

How

Esperanto and Polish share a lot of sounds. By converting Esperanto text into phonetic Polish, Parol leverages existing Text-To-Speech (TTS) engines to produce Esperanto speech. You can customise the rules that Parol uses by clicking on the settings icon.

Why

I'm building a language learning app called Yakk. As part of Yakk, I need to generate audio content for Esperanto courses. I couldn't find a TTS engine for Esperanto, so I created one.

Who

I'm Martin and I like building things. Here's my blog, GitHub, Twitter.

Where

Parol is composed of 3 components: this website, the background API, and the transcription engine. The code for all three are open source, and available at: https://git.io/fjb5n
